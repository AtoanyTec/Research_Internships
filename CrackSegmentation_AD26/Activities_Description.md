# 📄 Research Internship Description  
## Crack and Structural Damage Segmentation in Bridges using Convolutional Neural Networks

---

## 📅 General Information

- **Start date:** August 10, 2026  
- **End date:** December 4, 2026  
- **Duration:** 17 weeks  
- **Modality:** Applied research and technological development  
- **Monitoring:** Weekly progress seminars  

---

## 🎯 Objectives

### General Objective  
To develop a system based on convolutional neural networks (CNNs) for the detection and segmentation of cracks and structural damage in bridge infrastructures, contributing to automated structural monitoring.

### Specific Objectives  
- Understand the fundamentals of computer vision and semantic segmentation  
- Analyze relevant datasets of damaged structural images  
- Implement and train convolutional neural network models (e.g., U-Net and modern variants)  
- Evaluate model performance using metrics such as IoU and Dice Score  
- Develop a full-stack application including frontend and backend for model inference  
- Deploy the solution in the cloud for public access  
- Document the methodology and results in a scientific paper
